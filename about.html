Who Are We?

Callida Freemont RiskCheck (CFRC) is a UK/Nigerian defence and security risk management consultancy firm. We are providers of high-tech defence, security and risk management solutions to the armed forces, law enforcement, paramilitary, corporate bodies and industrial market. We aim to support the government security forces with appropriate training, systems and solutions targeted at increasing their capacity to operate and improve their safety, efficiency and survivability during operations. We also offer a range of tailored risk and security management services aimed at assessing the threats an individual or company faces. Whether they work in hostile and challenging environments or in more benign countries, we aim to manage the threats and ensure business operations can continue. Through our extensive network and our experienced team, we are able to guide and support our clients throughout the lifecycle of their project, whether they are new to the market, have an established presence incountry or are looking to expand their operations into new territories.

Defence Solutions

The increased capacity of aggressors, access to funds, usage of technologies, and the asymmetric nature of emerging combats continues to make the battle feed more challenging for the military and government forces, creating the need for superior, innovative solutions and technologies capable of increasing their capacity and enhancing their ability to efficiently conquer their enemy and effectively complete missions. The needs vary from survivability, specialised equipment, weapons, body armour, mobility and force protection technologies, amongst others.

Protecting businesses and individuals

CFRC offers a wide range of services to support businesses and private clients. Our dedicated and experienced team have in-depth knowledge of the risks posed to operations across the country. We have an extensive network of local safety and security contacts that can assist us in any project within the country. We make sure that client's business, operations, assets, staff and reputation are protected and provide the peace of mind required to carry on business as usual.

The Risk environment

The risks posed to businesses and their staff is constantly changing. Threats from war, supply chain disruption, terrorism, natural catastrophes, crime, corruption and cyberattacks mean that businesses must be prepared and provide a duty of care to their staff living and working in complex environments.
