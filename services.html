What We Do?

Pre-entry/Market Entry

CFRC can support organisations looking to enter a country, providing threat assessments, staff training, support for procurement of local logistics and security services as well as journey management services and site selection services.

Security Plan Development

CFRC are experts in developing bespoke security plans that take into consideration the specific risks faced by the organisation, the site and the operations they are undertaking. We follow UN Security Policies and ISO 31000 standards to ensure our clients are compliant and able to provide assurances to any stakeholders, investors or regulatory bodies.

Incident Tracker and Alerts

The Interactive Incident Tracker (InT) is not just a data base of security incidents in Nigeria and across Africa, but an online security information system, providing clients with real-time GIS security alerts including analysis and advisory post-incidents.
